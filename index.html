Our Mission

There are a lot of industries in the World. Every industry output gives scrap. There are two kind of scrap according to the perception of ECOSCRAP. One is useful scrap such as metal scrap which are already recycling and utilizing to get finished product and second is useless scrap which is neither recycling nor utilizing to get finished product so industries have to dump this kind of scrap and spending money in dumping.

ECOSCRAP Mission is to carry out research on useless scrap and let the world know how to recycle and utilize useless scrap in order to get finished product according to rules and regulations of manufacturing laws in order to keep environment safe. When ECOSCRAP will success in its Mission then world knows that how to utilize useless scrap and people will make money and they have new businesses to do.
